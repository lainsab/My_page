Building my own website

I started creating my own website as a project in a course during my studies at Haaga-Helia, University od Applied Sciences 2023.

The goal of the project was to learn the basics of designing a mobile-first webpage development. You could for example a company you wanted to build the page for, or then create a page for yourself, to act as portfolio of your work.

I chose the latter, and desided to work on building my own website.

I started my project with a very simple and fast sketch of how I would like the page to look like, and then continued to do a high-fidelity version on Figma
